Who and why are we?

We are a new restaurant chain in Finland created from the world of H.P. Lovecraft. Our goal is to create mythological food which tastes divine even when the food might seem suspicious to unknown eye. It's still just the theme of our restaurant! We'll guarantee you that when you dare to enter our restaurant, our service, atmosphere and food will wake the discussion and hopefully make you thirst for our food again and again!
